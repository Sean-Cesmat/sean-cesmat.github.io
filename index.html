Projects




Junction Flats located in West Seattle is my biggest solo project, that I’m very proud of. I did both the branding for the building along with a customized WordPress install making it easy for them to keep listings up to date. The logo has a bit of history connected to it. It is an illustration of the Alaska Street Junction tracks that were located a block away from the building is now. The font and the colors were chosen to give it a classy and aged feel to go along with the history in the name & logo. To follow the theme, the builders added railroad ties with hanging light bulbs.
